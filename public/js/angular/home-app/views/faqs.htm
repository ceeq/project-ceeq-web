Frequently asked questions


  • Why Ceeq doesn't has any antivirus feature ?
  • Android is not prone to viruses, it is a bit prone to malwares but Google is taking care of that too. Don't worry, and ignore sofwares which say they save you from viruses.

  • Why enable GPS ?
  • GPS is required for accuracy while getting the location of device, in distress situations or while using the application.

  • Why enable Device Administrator ?
  • Device administrator feature helps the application to wipe, lock your device remotely.

  • What are Remote Commands ?
  • Remote Commands are messages which you may send in case your device is lost.

    To use a remote command, type the remote command, followed by your pin ex. CEEQ LOCATE 000000.

    The main remote commands are
    1. CEEQ ALARM : sounds a loud alarm on your mobile,
    2. CEEQ LOCK : locks your device,
    3. CEEQ ERASE : completely erases your device of any data,
    4. CEEQ NOW : sends you the current sim number, imsi number and other mobile details,
    5. CEEQ CALLS : sends you the last 10 called numbers,
    6. CEEQ SPY : spies on the device location and calls and send them to you,
    7. CEEQ LOCATE : sends you the last stored location, and any new location if location of mobile changes.

  • Why so many permissions ?
  • We have explained each and every permission here.
    Still, in a one line, they are just required to give you more number of features.

  • What is Protect Me ?
  • Protect me feature has been added for distress situations, just double tap power button and rapidly tap Home button to send an SOS message to your emergency contacts.
    The same message will be posted to your connected social accounts too.

  • What is Online Sync ?
  • Online Sync, saves your backup data over Google Drive, which are used to restore data if in case you loose your phone or SD card.

  • What is One Click Backup
  • One click backup button simply backs up everything i.e contacts, messages, call logs, etc just by one click.

  • What is Stealth Mode ?
  • Stealth Mode hides the application icon & its notifications.
    Don't worry you can still launch the application by dialing *#*#2337#*#*. Note, it won't stop the background processes such as auto backups, or device tracking.
